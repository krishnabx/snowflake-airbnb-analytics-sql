# Insights Summary (Highlights)

1) Demand hotspots
- Certain neighborhoods show consistently higher occupancy proxy (booked pressure).

2) Strong seasonality in listed pricing
- Most neighborhoods peak in summer months and dip in winter months.

3) Weekend vs weekday patterns vary by neighborhood
- Some neighborhoods show weekend pricing premiums without equivalent occupancy uplift.

4) Declining demand diagnostics
- A subset of listings shows QoQ occupancy drops; these can be investigated with review engagement and recency.
